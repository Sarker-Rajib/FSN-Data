FSN21315T: Instrumental Methods in Food Analysis-I

Objectives:

This course will expose learners to

  • Principle and methods of UV-Vis, IR, NMR and Mass spectroscopes;
  • Methods of extraction and purification.

Learning Outcomes:

Upon completion of this course, the learners would be able to

  • Escribes the principle and methods of UV-Vis, IR, NMR and Mass spectroscopes;
  • Analysis of UV-Vis, IR, NMR and Mass spectroscopic data;
  • Extract and purify bioactive compounds from foods.
